Project 8 Seconds

All Profits Support the Cowboy Community

As sponsors of the Professional Bull Riders (PBR) the owners of Real Time Pain Relief had the opportunity to work very closely with the Cowboy Community. They noticed three issues that the general public is not aware of. 

  1. While very capable, the Cowboys can’t do everything.  Indeed, participating in Western Sports is, more often than not, a fiscally losing proposition.  The cost’s of travel, specialized equipment, entry fees, and time away from their families and ranches is significant.  Meanwhile, their success hinges on staying healthy as they try to stay on the back of a bucking bull, 8 seconds at a time.  The conditioning and training that requires, also take its toll.

  2. Many Rodeos and Bull Riding Events (especially the smaller ones) are struggling to survive financially.  Many times these are businesses not focused on a profit, but rather the preservation of a culture and community.

  3. The Riders Relief Fund is an important part of the Cowboy Community, yet funding is insufficient to meet the needs of the community.

Seeing an opportunity to help meet these needs, the owners of Real Time Pain Relief made three significant commitments to the Cowboy Community immediately following the 2015 PBR World Finals in Las Vegas:

First, they committed to become the National Sponsor of the Riders Relief Fund for the next 5 years.  Not only did they commit to a substantial financial donation, but also a commitment to help raise awareness, partnerships, and expand the community.

Second, they established Project 8 Seconds to highlight needs in the Cowboy Community, and to meet those needs by working with the community to raise money, educate, and bring awareness.

Third, they committed to triple their promotional budget for rodeos, riders and events in 2016 and to increase that budget 10-fold within the next three years.

History

Rodeo is an important part of the American West’s History.  The skills developed by Real Cowboys working Real Ranches, Calf Roping, Saddle Bronc, Steer Wrestling, and yes, even Bull Riding, are staples of the Rodeo torn directly from the pages of the history of the American West.  Project 8 Seconds provides an opportunity for businesses and individuals alike to keep this American Tradition alive by supporting those who dedicate their lives to this important sport.
